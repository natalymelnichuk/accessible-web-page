About Us

"Shanti" is a sanctuary where time slows down. We have created a space entirely free from urban noise and distraction, allowing you to focus completely on what truly matters — yourself.

Our certified instructors will mindfully guide you from basic foundational asanas to advanced meditation practices. We firmly believe yoga is accessible to everyone, regardless of age or flexibility.

Our Services

A classical practice focused on strengthening the body, improving alignment, and breath control. Perfect for beginners.

Yin Yoga

A deep, meditative practice of deep relaxation. Holding poses longer helps release deep tension and calm the nervous system.

Meditation

Breathing techniques and mindful exercises to clear the mind, reduce daily stress, and cultivate inner peace.
